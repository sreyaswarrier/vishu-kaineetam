# 💸 The Digital Kaineetam Request Protocol v1.0

### "Tradition is great, but UPI is faster."

Let’s be real: physical cash is a logistical nightmare in 2026. Carrying around ₹10 notes is outdated, and waiting for your uncles to find their wallets is inefficient. This project is a highly over-engineered solution to a problem that didn't really exist.

It’s a 1:1 interactive card designed to guilt-trip your relatives into sending you money via GPay. 

## 🌼 Why did I make this?
1. **The Cash Crisis:** Nobody carries physical change anymore.
2. **Efficiency:** Why wait for a hug when you can just send a QR code?
3. **Boredom:** I had some Tailwind classes lying around and a dream of financial gain.

## 🛠️ "Cutting Edge" Tech Stack
* **HTML5:** Because you need tags to hold your begging Bowl.
* **Tailwind CSS:** To make the yellow (#FACC15) look "intentional" and "aesthetic" rather than "I just picked the first yellow I saw."
* **Pure Vibes:** The core engine driving this entire repository.

## 🚀 Features (if you can call them that)
* **1:1 Aspect Ratio:** Specifically designed so it looks like a "professional" post while you're actually just asking for money.
* **Kanikkonna Yellow™:** Optimized to trigger the "Vishu reflex" in Malayalis.
* **Hover Effects:** The card glows. Does it add value? No. Does it look cool? Yes.
* **Zero Backend:** Because why would I need a database to store my lack of funds?

## 📖 How to "Deploy" your Begging
1. Open `index.html`.
2. Find the part where I left a gap for the GPay number.
3. Put your number in.
4. Send the link to the family WhatsApp group.
5. Profit? (Results may vary depending on how much your family likes you).

This project is a joke. Please don't actually get disowned by your family for being "too digital." If you do, don't blame the code—blame the hustle.
